Meet Our Team

Reinalyn Lee, Care Coordinator

Reinalyn is a proud member of the jump6 team.  You will find her always with a smile and happy to greet you as you begin your weight loss journey.

Reinalyn is a patient coordinator who is always willing to assist you with coordinating your procedure appointments and follow up care.  She is enthusiastic and always willing to go the extra mile to ensure your experience is prompt, courteous, and positive.

She looks forward to meeting you as you begin your very own weight loss success story.

 

 

 

 

Susan Lam

Susan operates at Jump6 as a Health Coach where she is passionate about empowering and improving the health and wellness of patients to achieve their personal health goals through a holistic health care approach.

Prior to joining Jump6, Susan operated as a Health Educator and Program Manager assisting a large network of family physicians in the Greater Toronto Area in helping their patients living with chronic diseases to better self manage, and to achieve their personal health goals. She also worked as a Nutritionist in several rehabilitative clinics and fitness facilities in Toronto, and was also the Director of Nutritional Services at Activa Clinics where she gained experience in group education, nutritional program development and management.

Susan has a Bachelor of Science (BScN) in Health Studies from the University of Toronto, and Certification of Nutrition from George Brown College (C.N.P).

Susan looks forward to assisting you in your desired weight loss goals in a positive, healthy way.

Punya Puri

Punya Puri is a Toronto based Registered Dietitian and a Certified Diabetes Educator who has experience helping her clients meet their health goals. She has a master’s degree in public health, specializing in nutrition, from Ryerson University Toronto and she is a member of Dietitians of Canada and the College of Dietitians of Ontario. She also has a Certified Diabetes Educator license from Canadian Diabetes Association of Canada. In busy downtown Toronto practice, Punya counsels individuals and families on a wide range of health issues, with particular focus on healthy lifestyle issues and chronic disease management – healthy weight loss, diabetes, renal and cardiac issues, digestive concerns and aging well.

Over her career she has developed skills and a special interest in working collaboratively with psychologists and physicians regarding emotional eating and other chronic diseases. Punya has a trusted ability to encourage her clients to eat more mindfully, intuitively and overall improve their relationship with food and their body.
Punya also did presentations for Eat Right Ontario and appeared on South Asian television focusing on diabetes amongst immigrants. She also does various community outreach programs in East Toronto for marginalized and urban population and runs a successful Community Kitchen for food security. In addition, she writes nutrition blog on latest food trends and healthy recipes.

Punya has always lived her life with a profound respect for the power of lifestyle in managing disease prevention, maximizing health and energy, and maintaining a healthy weight. She would be happy to assist you in ensuring your lifestyle is helping you live your best life.

Dr. Bianca Drennan, BSc, ND Naturopathic Doctor BSc in Nutritional Sciences

Bianca has been an advocate of long-term health and wellness for much of her life. Growing up in an Italian family gave her an appreciation for how food and lifestyle are connected. From planting a garden with her grandfather to cooking traditional Italian dishes with her grandmother, Bianca learned early on that diet had a significant impact on health.

Bianca followed her passion for diet and health to McGill University, where she graduated with a Bachelor of Science in Nutritional Sciences. Specializing in sports nutrition, Bianca moved her passion into a career by studying the relationship between what we eat and how our bodies perform through the lens of science. Hooked by the potential to help people with day-to-day health and wellness by working on the root cause, and not the symptoms, of health issues, Bianca completed the four-year post-graduate Bachelor of Naturopathy program at the Canadian College of Naturopathic Medicine (CCNM).

CCNM is one of two board-certified naturopathic medical schools in Canada. During her time at CCNM, she interned at the Robert Schad Naturopathic Clinic - the largest naturopathic clinic in Canada. During her internship, she interned at Sherbourne Health Center, treating patients with HIV/AIDS. She also interned at Queen West Community Health Centre, which focused on treating patients with mental health conditions. Bianca has treated a wide-range of health concerns, but has special interests in diabetes, weight loss, stress management, and digestive conditions. Bianca practices evidence-based nutritional and naturopathic medicine combined with clinical experience to successfully guide her patients towards optimal health.

Bianca can work with you in achieving your goals, which will lead you toward optimal health. She is excited to work with the Jump6 Weight Loss Program to help clients achieve longterm wellness.

Memberships:  Bianca is a licensed Naturopathic Doctor and is regulated under the Regulated Health Professions Act (RHPA). She is a member of the Ontario Association of Naturopathic Doctors (OAND) and the Canadian Association of Naturopathic Doctors (CAND).

Joanne Zhou, RD Registered Dietitian

I am a Registered Dietitian with a degree from McGill University in Nutritional Sciences. My clinical nutrition training has taken place in a community health centre, ICU and Oncology Outpatient settings in a hospital, COPD rehab centre and a long-term care facility. I have a special interest area in wellness innovations and open to alternative strategies you may bring.

Born in Shanghai, raised in Tokyo and Sydney, I now live in Toronto. It was through working at a school garden that rooted me in my passion for food. Picking crisp kale and eating fresh cherry tomatoes made me appreciate healthy food and inspired my eventual study of nutrition.

What fulfills me the most is when I help others realize their dreams. Even just one small goal met can create positive changes that trickle into other areas of life. What we eat directly impacts our future health and is empowering to know we can do something about it.

Those with experience in the often frustrating world of weight loss are well aware of healthy eating. What we need most is someone to give us the tools to build habits. In this way, I see my role as more of a lifestyle coach rather than simply an educator. I will be supporting you for your whole Jump6 program. In our sessions together we will come up with small goals that grow into larger ones. Making progress no matter how small helps in growing long-term healthy habits that stick.

Other than nutrition, my interests include experimenting with new recipes, eating locally, contemporary art, photography and tennis. I have an active social media presence on topics related to nutrition and innovation. You can follow me on twitter @jwnzhou.

Specialties: weight loss, diabetes prevention, COPD, ICU, oncology, Parkinson’s, geriatrics and anemia.

Memberships: Licensed Registered Dietitian with the College of Dietitians of Ontario

Robyn Barefoot, DDEPT, MSc(c), RD Registered Dietitian

Robyn Barefoot is a Registered Dietitian in the Greater Toronto Area who has a passion for helping clients succeed with and maintain their weight management goals. Robyn is a proud graduate from Western University and went on to pursue her diploma in Dietetic Education and Practical Training. As a curious nutrition practitioner, she is currently pursuing her Master’s of Science at The University of Guelph, specializing in Human Health and Nutritional Science. Throughout this degree she is focussing on weight management and overall health and wellbeing in children and families. Robyn has a variety of clinical training including surgical and non-surgical bariatric treatment and weight management in complex mental illness, in addition to her internship experience in diabetes, cardiology, geriatrics and general medicine.

Robyn enjoys educating around mindful eating. She understands healthy eating can be challenging and enjoys working with her clients to help explore why we eat the way we do. Robyn aims to help her clients in developing the healthiest relationship with food as possible.

Robyn translates her knowledge and passion for the field through the development of articles and blog posts for numerous organizations on weight management. She enjoys cooking, exploring new recipes and has a depth of knowledge in the kitchen including strong food skills she enjoys sharing with her clients. Robyn looks forward to helping you with your nutrition goals in order to succeed in living a long, healthy and happy lifestyle.

Memberships: Robyn is a licensed Registered Dietitian and is regulated under the Regulated Health Professions Act (RHPA) and the Dietetics Act. She is a member of the College of Dietitians of Ontario (CDO).
