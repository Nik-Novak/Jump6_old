Weight Loss and Fat Burning Smoothies

 An easy way to burn fat!

  

Fat Burner Smoothies

  • 2 handfuls of greens
  • 2 cups cool green tea
  • ½ cup coconut milk
  • juice of 1 lemon
  • ¼ pitted dates
  • ½ avocado peeled and pitted
  • ½ pink grapefruit peeled and seeded

 

Orange Banana Greens

  • 2 handful of greens
  • ½ cup of water
  • 2 oranges peeled and seeded
  • 2 bananas peeled and frozen

 

Berry Pears

  • 2 handful of greens
  • 1 ½ almond milk
  • 2 cups frozen mixed berries
  • 2 pears seeded

 

Banana Berry Almond

  • 2 handful of greens
  • 1 ½ cup almond milk
  • 1 banana peeled and frozen
  • 1 cup frozen blueberries
  • ½ cup frozen strawberries

 

Berry Cantaloupe

  • 2 handful of greens
  • 1 cup of water
  • ½ cantaloupe peeled and seeded
  • 1 ½ cup frozen strawberries

 

Cherry Orange

  • 2 handful of greens
  • 1 ½ cup almond milk
  • 1 cup frozen cherries
  • 2 oranges peeled and seeded
  • 1 tablespoon chia seeds

 

Raspberry Orange

  • 2 handful of greens
  • 1 ½ cup of water
  • 2 oranges peeled and seeded
  • 2 cups frozen raspberries

 

Peachy Vanilla

  • 2 handful of greens
  • 1 cup of water
  • 1 ½ cup frozen peaches
  • 1 cup frozen strawberries
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

 

Mango Lime

  • 2 handful of greens
  • 1 ½ cup water
  • 1 orange peeled and seeded
  • ½ cup frozen mango chunks
  • 1 lime peeled and seeded
  • 1 packet of stevia ( optional)

 

Green Raspberry

  • 2 handful of greens
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 banana peeled and frozen
  • 1 cup frozen raspberries
  • 2 tablespoon of ground flax

 

Chia Pear

  • 2 handful of greens
  • 1 ½ cup water
  • 1 banana peeled and frozen
  • 2 pears
  • 2 tablespoon of chia seeds

 

Pineapple Orange Greens

  • 2 handful of greens
  • 1 cup ice
  • 1 cup pineapple chunks
  • 2 oranges peeled and seeded

 

Watermelon Greens

  • 2 handful of greens
  • 1 cup ice
  • 2 cups watermelon
  • 1 tablespoon flax seeds

 

Pineapple Orange Greens

  • 2 handful of greens
  • 1 cup ice
  • 1 cup pineapple chunks
  • 2 oranges peeled and seeded

 

Grapefruit Pineapple

  • 2 handful of greens
  • ½ cup coconut water
  • ½ cup ice
  • 1 cup pineapple chunks
  • 1 pink grapefruit peeled and seeded

 

Which Greens and Why?

Arugula: A great source of folic acid, as well as vitamin A, C, and K, provides boost for bone and brain health. Zippy , Peppery Flavor.

Beet Greens: Leafy tops to beet vegetables, rich in Vitamin K, known to help improve vision and boost immune system.

Bok Choy: Chinese cabbage that is mild and crunchy, full of vitamin A, C ,calcium, and antioxidants.

Chard: Green , leafy with red stalks , leaf veins and stems. Beet like taste and mild texture, known to help prevent cancer and is good for cleansing and the digestive system.

Collard Greens: Green and leafy, similar to kale but chewier and has a very strong taste. They are a superior agent in binding to bile acids throughout the digestive tract. good at lowering cholesterol.

Kale: Lightweight and loaded with vitamins A,C, and K, known for lowering colon, breast, and ovarian cancer.

Lettuce: Contains essential amino acids and vitamins, be sure to choose dark and leafy for higher nutritional value.

Mustard Greens: Effective in lowering cholesterol and provides healthy dose of riboflavin, niacin, magnesium, and iron.

Parsley: Rich in antioxidants, minerals, vitamins, and fiber, known for reducing aging and helps regulate blood sugar.

Spinach: A leafy green, mild tasting, packed with high levels of Omega 3’s, calcium, vitamins A, C, E, and K.
