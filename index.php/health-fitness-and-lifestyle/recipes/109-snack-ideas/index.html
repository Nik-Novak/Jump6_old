Snack Ideas

Check out some amazing and fun snack ideas. 

 

Apple Peanut butter Cookies( Love these!)

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 apple
  • ¼ cup peanut butter ( Natural almond butter,nut butter)
  • ¼ cup almonds, sliced
  • ¼ cup walnuts, chopped
  • ¼ cup shredded coconut
  • ¼ cup chocolate chips

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Slice apple into thing rings and remove core.
  2. Spread peanut butter over one side of ring. Top with almonds, walnuts, coconut, and chocolate chips.

Frozen Greek Yogurt Blueberries

Stick a berry with a bamboo skewer (a toothpick would work too) and dip/swirl it in the yogurt.Then ease it off the end of the skewer and onto a cookie sheet. It’s easier to do this if you just barely stick the berry onto the skewer. Keep dipping and swirling until all of the blueberries are gone or you run out of yogurt  …and put them in the freezer.  Literally like 10 or 15 minutes. But  recommended is leaving them in for an hour initially and then pop them off the cookie sheet and into a plastic container or baggie.

Sweet and Spicey Chic Pea Crunch     

2 (15 oz.) cans chickpeas

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon cumin
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/8 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1-2 tablespoons brown sugar

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees. Rinse and drain chickpeas; dry well on paper towels.
  2. Place chickpeas on a lightly greased baking sheet and bake for 10 minutes. Remove pan from oven ad shake. Return to oven for another 10 minutes.
  3. Meanwhile, combine seasonings and brown sugar in a small bowl.
  4. Remove chickpeas from oven and drizzle with olive oil. Sprinkle with spices and shake pan again to coat chickpeas with olive oil and seasonings. Return to oven and bake chickpeas for another 10-15 minutes, or until chickpeas are browned and very crunchy. You can also simplify and just use plain sea salt and they make such a great snack.

 

Oat Squares

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 cup peanut butter ( organic or nut butter, Almond butter optional)
  • ½ cup honey (raw organic and melt)
  • 3 cups rolled oats

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Line a 9x9 pan with foil. Spray lightly with non-stick spray.
  2. Melt the peanut butter and honey together until smooth, either in the microwave or stove top. Combine mixture with oats.
  3. Press into prepared pan. Place in refrigerator until set. Cut into squares.

NOTE: keep in fridge

Cucumber Sandwiches

Ingredients:

  • cucumbers
  • tomatoes
  • avocado
  • sliced chicken or turkey
  • sliced cheese
  • sea salt
  • toothpicks

Instructions:

Begin by slicing the cucumber, tomato, and avocado into 1/4″ thick slices.  You can add other veggies too if you like.  I think red onion would be delicious!

Cut the sliced chicken into 1/2″ wide pieces.

Layer the veggies, cheese, and meat between the two cucumber slices and hold together with a toothpick. Add a bit of sea salt to tomato and cucumber and stack them.

Tomato avocado Melt

Ingredients

  1. 4 slices of bread (gluten free or whole wheat,weight watchers ) I also use rounds non GMO
  2. 1-2 Tbsp mayonnaise
  3. cayenne pepper, to taste
  4. 1 small Roma tomato, sliced thin
  5. 1/2 avocado, sliced thin
  6. 8 slices of cheese (your favorite kind)

Instructions

  1. Spread a thin layer or mayonnaise on each piece of bread. Sprinkle with a pinch of cayenne pepper. Layer bread with tomato and avocado slices and top with cheese. Broil on high for 2-4 minutes, or until bread is toasted and cheese is bubbly!
