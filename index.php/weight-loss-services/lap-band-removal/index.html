Lap Band Removal

WELCOME TO THE LAP BAND RESCUE PROGRAM: Do you need your Lap Band Removed or have you had an unsuccessful Lap Band Surgery?

If you are struggling with your lap band or have had unsuccessful weight loss with your band, the Jump6 program in Toronto can assist you today with achieving your desired weight loss goals.

Our fully trained bariatric surgeon is here to assist you, call 647-667-7473 for a complimentary consult or CLICK OUR FORM to receive more information.

Unfortunately lap band removal is common in some cases and a certain percentage of all patients eventually have their bands removed. Some patients have their bands removed due to serious complications such as erosion, infection, slippage or intolerance.

If you are still looking to achieve your weight loss goals and have been unsuccessful with your band, the lap band rescue program with jump6 can assist you. If you are looking to explore your weight loss options please call 647-667-7473 today and one of our patient coordinators will be happy to assist you with any questions you may have.
